The Risk of Acetyldehyde

1. Inhaled


Low-concentration steam can cause cough with burning of the nose and throat. Higher concentrations may lead to delayed pulmonary edema beginning with symptoms of joy followed by narcosis accompanied by symptoms of headache, drowsiness, dizziness. Other effects include severe irritation of the mucous membranes, throat, chest pain, bronchitis, difficulty breathing, nausea, vomiting, malaise
